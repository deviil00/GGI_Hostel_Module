{# ── HOD Routing info banner ──────────────────────────────────── #}
How this drives leave routing: When a student applies for leave, the system checks the departure date in this calendar. If that date is marked as Working (or defaults to Mon–Fri if no entry exists), the leave is sent to the HOD of the student's department first, then forwarded to the Warden after HOD approval. Non-working days (holidays, exams, etc.) go directly to the Warden.
